Titanic Survivors

The visualization below gives an overview of the passengers who survived/perished the titanic shipwreck.
The dataset does not contain all the passengers who were onboard the titanic but only a small subset of all the passengers on the ship.
The visualization shows the breakdown of passengers by the class in which they were travelling.
First class is the highest passenger class in a ship and third class is the lowest.
